Raison D'etre means a passionate aspiration or ambition and can be a synonym for bucket list. This is an app for everyone who is looking for somewhere to save all spots in their city that they want to visit.

How to use:

  • You need to sign-up or login
  • You can check your list of places by clicking all places.
  • If you want to view all the details about a specific place you can do so by just clicking on the place.
  • If you want to add a new place you can click on add a place and fill the form
  • If you want to edit, delete a place you can do so by going to the detail page.
  • If you want to upload or change an image you can do so by going to the detail page
